Welcome to Elvfjord!

Elvfjord is a beautiful village located in the heart of Norway. Surrounded by majestic mountains and serene fjords, it offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The village is known for its friendly locals, traditional wooden houses, and stunning natural beauty.

Visitors to Elvfjord can enjoy a variety of outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, and kayaking. The nearby national park provides numerous trails that cater to all levels of hikers, offering breathtaking views of the landscape.

This year, discussions are underway about building hydropower stations in the valley near our village. The campaign has officially begun!
